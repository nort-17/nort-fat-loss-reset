Methodology and disclaimer

Calories are approximate averages from common nutrition databases and food labels. Values vary by brand, recipe, restaurant, cooking method, and portion size. This tool is for education and habit-building, not medical advice. For exact tracking, use the nutrition label or a food tracking app.

Food images are visual approximations. The app shows serving weights and calorie estimates to help you understand realistic portion swaps, but appearance can vary.

A lower-calorie swap is only useful if it still satisfies the same craving. That is why this tool scores swaps by satisfaction and repeatability, not just calories saved.
